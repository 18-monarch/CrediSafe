# CrediSafe Mobile Experience

The public homepage now uses two intentional compositions:

- **Desktop:** the existing cinematic, scroll-synchronised automotive presentation.
- **Mobile and tablet:** a native-scroll product story designed for touch, readability and fast comprehension.

## Mobile structure

1. Focused cinematic hero
2. Transparent score and XP proof
3. Complete journey-to-reward loop
4. Journey intelligence
5. Reward value
6. Working product capabilities
7. Positive recognition
8. Final product CTA

## Main improvements

- Added a dedicated 9:16 mobile video asset instead of aggressively cropping the desktop video.
- Reduced the mobile header to the logo and one clear action.
- Removed fixed desktop HUD overlays, chapter rails and scrubbed video behaviour from mobile.
- Disabled Lenis smooth scrolling on touch layouts and retained native mobile scrolling.
- Replaced compressed full-screen chapters with clean vertical sections and progressive disclosure.
- Added an explainable score and XP result card above the fold of the product story.
- Added clear working-capability proof without inventing traction or partner claims.
- Kept video observations reviewable and explicitly separated from automatic scoring.
- Improved safe-area padding, touch targets, typography and small-screen spacing.

## Breakpoint

The dedicated mobile composition is used at widths up to `900px`. The desktop cinematic experience remains active above that breakpoint.
