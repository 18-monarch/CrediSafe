# Funding Demo Script

## Demo preparation

- Use one clear registered vehicle.
- Record a short 5–15 second plate-visible clip.
- Keep a safe-city simulation ready as fallback.
- Confirm both web and Python health endpoints before the meeting.

## Eight-minute flow

1. Open the cinematic CrediSafe landing page.
2. Enter the rider application.
3. Show the registered vehicle.
4. Run or display a completed GPS trip.
5. Explain distance, speed, GPS quality and the transparent score.
6. Open Video Verification and select that trip.
7. Upload the prepared clip.
8. Show Python processing and the plate match.
9. Return to trip history to show linked evidence.
10. Show XP, rewards and leaderboard progression.

## Strong pitch line

> GPS tells us how the journey moved. Video helps verify which vehicle appears in the evidence. CrediSafe joins both into one transparent rider-safety record.

## Do not claim

Do not call the current vision layer a full traffic-violation detector. Say that plate matching is the validated first computer-vision capability and that custom helmet/phone/signal models are the next funded development step.
